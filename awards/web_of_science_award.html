Dr. Maxat Kassen's research awards:

Dr. Maxat Kassen received The Web of Science Award 2020: Leader of Science (Clarivate Analytics, USA)

In November 2020, Dr. Maxat Kassen received the prestigious Web of Science Award 2020: Leader of Science in the category “Publication Leader in Social Sciences” from Clarivate Analytics (USA). Clarivate is a globally renowned academic agency that indexes such a high reputable database of impact factor journals as Web of Science. This prize is awarded annually to prominent scholars around the world, whose contribution to the development of higher education and science is the most impactful.
