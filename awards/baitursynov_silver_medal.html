Dr. Maxat Kassen's research awards:


***

The Baitursynov Silver Medal 2017 (Almaty, Kazakhstan)

In December 2017, Dr. Maxat Kassen received the Ahmet Baitursynov Silver Medal for significant achievements in scientific research and excellence in teaching students. The prestigious award was given by the Association of Institutions of Higher Education (Almaty, Kazakhstan). The medal is widely regarded as one of the most prestigious research awards in Kazakhstan.
